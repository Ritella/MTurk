Let's listen to some stories!


In the following HIT, you will listen to four sound clips, each about 7 minutes long, all taken from entertainment sources (e.g. film, tv, radio). You'll answer some basic questions about each clip, like "Can you tell me what happened in this clip?"

This HIT should take under an hour to complete. We do ask that you complete this HIT in one sitting and that you do not multi-task while performing the HIT.

Therefore, there is a strict 2 hour timeout on this HIT. Please don't accept the HIT if you think you cannot dedicate an un-interrupted hour to this.

Please only complete this HIT once. (If you have already completed a HIT with this same name and requester, please don't do the HIT again.)

This HIT is for native English speakers only.

Please note: You will need headphones or speakers.

This survey is part of an ongoing research program at Johns Hopkins University and has been approved by the Johns Hopkins Institutional Review Board. Please read this form for additional information. By agreeing to this survey, you acknowledge that you have read the form, are participating voluntarily, and are 18 years of age or older and have normal or corrected-to-normal hearing. Your participation in this research is voluntary, and you may decline further participation without adverse consequences. Your anonymity is assured. If you have questions about this research, or if you would like to receive a report when it is completed, please contact the lab at plasticity_lab@jhu.edu.
